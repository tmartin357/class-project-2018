Live Stream

November 19, 2018 by Justin

Attention suscribers and gamers...on December 16th, We will be hosting our first press conference. If you're not in the Los Angeles area, we will be streaming live on Twitch and Youtube Live. We will cover topics about what to expect from us in 2019. We will explain each membership and what our goal is in order to keep our gamers satisfied. If you have any questions or ideas,you will be able to have your questions answered by leaving comments under our stream. Also, on December 19th, we will also be streaming gameplay of the upcoming video game, Kingdom Hearts 2, before it's official release. Tune in to our channel to watch us broadcast more gameplay for your entertainment.
