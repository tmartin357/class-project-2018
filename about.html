About Us

We LOVE video games. We eat, drink, sleep, and breath them every single day. But weâ€™re especially drawn to the Indie game, specifically developed by the small independent studio who produces games which are unique, original, fan-based, widely-varied, but (un)fortunately underrated.

Along with the great benefits of these games, smaller shops also experience many struggles. Some are listed below with:

  • Funding
  • Resources
  • Publicity

We realized there was a need here, to help the local and independent game developer become more successful in what they do. So we created the platform, Skyplay, to help resolve some of the challenges of the Indie developer. Through this company, we can expose a larger audience, who desires to play action, adventure, role-playing, simulation, strategy, and sports Indie games.

How we specifically do this: we pay a monthly fee to developers for access to each Indie game which we, in turn, upload to our servers. Making these games available to gamers on a wider basis, increases the profit potential for the developer, affording the smaller shop more resources to create even better games in the future.

We see our service as a support to the work of the ultimate geniuses of independent game development--- the designers, artists, sound engineers, programmers, and testers.

Skyplay is a platform created by gamers, in support of the Indie game developer and player.

We are Skyplay.
