FAQ

1. What is Skyplay?

Skyplay is an online site where you can stream video games from our servers. You also have the option to upload your own games with a higher level membership. Learn more.

2. How much does it cost?

There are three levels of membership: Platinum, Silver, and Gold which starts at $15 per month. Learn more.

3. How can I access Skyplay?

You can access Skyplay using any device that has a strong internet connection.

4. Can I change my membership plan?

Yes, at any time.

5. What are the requirements?

Must have an internet connection. Some games require being 18 years old or older.

6. Is there a free trial, demo, or rewards?

We offer Skyplay points: 5 points every 3 months and 2 points for following us on Facebook. There is no free trial, however, there are video trailers available for you to demo each game.

7. Console game refuses to work correctly. What should I do?

You do not need a console with Skyplay. We host the game and have a very effective, 24/7 support team available to assist you. Your progress is saved on our servers.

8. Fetch Quests - Gamers scroll through hoping to find rewards, instead they end up talking to random people and getting nothing.

Skyplay offers rewards automatically: 5 points for every 3 months of membership and 2 points when you follow/share (with) someone on Facebook. The rewards are redeemable for new gamers/demos.

9. I want to trade in a game and play it again. Is this possible?

The first class membership has an unlimited amount of hours, second class has 200 hours, and third class has 100 hours, where the customer may play and replay games as many times as they wish within the allowed number of hours.
